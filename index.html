Welcome to Rajalakshmi Engineering College, Chennai

Rajalakshmi Engineering College (REC), Chennai, is a leading institution of higher education in India. Established in 1997, REC has been committed to providing quality education and producing skilled professionals in various fields of engineering and technology.

Courses Offered:

  • B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering
  • B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering
  • B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering
  • B.Tech in Electrical and Electronics Engineering
  • B.Tech in Civil Engineering

Faculty Members:

Our college is proud to have a team of experienced and dedicated faculty members who are experts in their respective fields.

Facilities:

  • State-of-the-art laboratories
  • Modern classrooms with audio-visual aids
  • Library with a vast collection of books and online resources
  • Hostel accommodation for students
  • Sports facilities
  • Cafeteria serving nutritious food
